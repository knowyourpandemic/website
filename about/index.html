Amidst so much misinformation regarding the COVID 19 Pandemic, it's easily possible to be misled.

So, our team of talented & trained Doctors with experience of working with COVID positive patients decided to make one platform, which would be a place where all the accurate and up-to-date information could be accessed; which would be fun & simple to understand. Also having a goal to solve your doubts about COVID-19 and also guide you to take the appropriate steps.

Information found throughout this website is sourced and reported from credible institutions, organizations and, health authorities like the WHO, CDC, MoHFW, ICMR, NIH. You can find more information by clicking any of the links mentioned in the 'References' section.

With our experience of working with COVID positive patients, we promise to guide you in staying healthy during this pandemic.

Disclaimer: This website is for informational purposes only, it is not to be considered as a substitute to a health expert's or medical professional's advice. Seek a medical professional for personalized consultation regarding your health.

You may also ask us your doubts regarding COVID-19 for free in the 'Doubts' section.

Or Contact us on the following email: knowyourpandemic@gmail.com

Or Ping us on one of our social media accounts.

Symbolism of Our Logo:

For the writers it is the symbol of being done. They put a dot when they are done with their sentence.

For the developers it is the symbol of exploration.

For us a dot is that we have done the scouring of information for you and have brought it at one place.

The circle and block symbolic for a magnifying glass, to look into 2 things:

The DETAILS of this DISEASE and it's PREVENTION and CURE.
